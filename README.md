# Privacy Policy for GrenCam

**Last Updated:** 21/4/2026

---

GrenCam uses your device's camera solely for real-time scene lighting analysis to calculate optimal camera settings for FPV missions. We do not record, store, or transmit any images or video data from your device.

In the **Free version**, Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads.

No other personal data is collected, stored, or shared. All analytics are performed locally on your device.
